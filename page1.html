A bubble plot is a scatter plot with a third numeric variable mapped to circle size. This slice x axis is the number of confirmed case, y axis is number of deaths, and the circle size is the tested case, press 'Play' button the page will show you the overview of Canada COVID-19 disease overview, the slice is demonstrating the change from March, 2020 to July, 2020, it is easy to find out the top N provinces with deaths number and confirmed case.
